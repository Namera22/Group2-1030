Smart Transport Systems Engineer

Reference Number: ST001

Join our Transport & Mobility team to design, implement, and maintain intelligent transport monitoring systems across metropolitan and regional councils. You will work directly with transport authorities and local government partners to deliver real-time traffic management and public transit solutions.

Position Details

Salary: $90,000 – $110,000 per annum + superannuation

Reporting Line: Reports to the Director of Smart Infrastructure

Employment Type: Full-time, Permanent

Location: Melbourne, VIC (Hybrid)

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design and deploy intelligent transport monitoring systems for council partners.
  2. Integrate real-time traffic data feeds into urban management dashboards.
  3. Collaborate with transport authorities to identify system improvement opportunities.
  4. Conduct regular performance audits of deployed transport infrastructure.
  5. Prepare technical documentation, reports, and presentations for stakeholders.
  6. Ensure all systems comply with relevant safety and accessibility standards.

Essential Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or related field.
  • Minimum 3 years experience in transport systems, ITS, or smart infrastructure.
  • Proficiency in network communications protocols (TCP/IP, MQTT, REST APIs).
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills in complex system environments.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills for stakeholder reporting.

Preferable Requirements

  • Experience working with Australian state or local government transport agencies.
  • Familiarity with GIS mapping tools and spatial data analysis platforms.
  • Knowledge of SCATS or SCOOT traffic signal control systems.
  • Postgraduate qualification in Smart Cities, Urban Engineering, or similar.
